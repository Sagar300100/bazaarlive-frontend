## 1. Platform Overview

Any & All (anynall.com, operated by Any&All Private Limited) is India's live
auction marketplace. Sellers run live video shows; buyers bid and buy in real
time. Payment is held in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

## 2. Who Can Sell

- Indian residents aged 18+ with valid Aadhaar + PAN
- Registered businesses (proprietorship, LLP, Pvt Ltd) with GST if applicable
- Individual creators / resellers (Instagram thrift stores, sneaker collectors, etc.) — welcome

Sellers must complete KYC before going live.

## 3. KYC Requirements

| Required | How |
|---|---|
| Aadhaar verification | Via DigiLocker on signup (one-time) |
| PAN verification | Via Razorpay (during payment setup) |
| Bank account | Linked to PAN; verified via penny drop |
| GST | Only if annual turnover > ₹20 lakh (₹40 lakh for goods) |

Account name and Aadhaar name MUST match. Sellers cannot bypass this.

## 4. Fees

| Item | Rate |
|---|---|
| Platform fee on each sale | **8%** of final sale price |
| Razorpay gateway charge | 2% + GST (pass-through to seller) |
| Listing fee | **Free** for first 100 sellers; ₹0 thereafter |
| Show hosting fee | **Free** |
| Monthly subscription | **None** |
| Withdrawal fee | **None** (Razorpay handles payout) |

Example: a seller sells an item for ₹1,000.
- Platform fee: ₹80 (8%)
- Razorpay gateway: ₹23.60 (2% + 18% GST on the ₹20)
- Seller receives: ₹896.40 (89.6% of sale)

## 5. Allowed Products

Examples (non-exhaustive):
- Apparel (new and pre-loved)
- Sneakers and shoes
- Watches, jewellery, accessories
- Collectibles (sports cards, comics, coins, stamps)
- Beauty (sealed, original)
- Electronics in retail packaging up to ₹50,000 per item
- Home decor, art
- Books, vinyl records

## 6. Banned Products

Sellers may NOT list any of these. Listing them results in immediate account
suspension AND forfeit of pending payouts.

- Weapons, ammunition, or replicas
- Drugs, alcohol, tobacco, vapes (controlled substances)
- Counterfeit goods (fake branded items, replica designer)
- Stolen goods or goods of suspect provenance
- Adult / sexual content
- Live animals or animal parts (ivory, fur from endangered species)
- Government-issued documents (passports, Aadhaar cards, etc.)
- Currency, lottery tickets, gambling chips
- Items requiring prescription (medicines, contact lenses)
- Hazardous materials (chemicals, fireworks, explosives)
- Anything illegal under Indian law

When in doubt, sellers should email hello@anynall.com BEFORE listing.

## 7. Listing & Product Guidelines

- Photos must be of the actual item (no stock photos for pre-loved goods)
- Title must accurately describe the item
- Condition must be honestly stated (New / Like New / Used / Refurbished)
- Authenticity claims must be backed by receipts or expert verification on request

## 8. Shipping

- Seller is responsible for shipping
- Must dispatch within **48 hours** of buyer's payment clearing
- Must provide tracking number within 24 hours of dispatch
- Recommended partners: Delhivery, Bluedart, Shiprocket
- Buyer pays shipping unless seller offers free shipping
- Buyer should receive item within **7 working days** for metros, 10 for elsewhere

## 9. Returns

Buyer can request return within **7 days of delivery** if:
- Item not as described
- Damaged on arrival
- Wrong item received

Buyer canNOT return for change of mind unless seller's listing offered "no-questions returns".

### Return shipping cost
- If seller is at fault → seller pays
- If buyer is at fault (wrong size, change of mind) → buyer pays

## 10. Refunds

- Refund issued within **3 working days** of returned item received by seller
- Refund goes to original payment method
- Disputed cases → see Section 13

## 11. Escrow & Payouts

- Buyer pays at point of sale
- Payment held in escrow for **7 days post-delivery** (gives buyer time to inspect)
- If no dispute raised in 7 days → automatically released to seller
- Seller withdraws to linked bank account; Razorpay processes in **T+1 working day**

## 12. Cancellations

- Seller may cancel before dispatch if item unavailable (rare; will damage seller rating)
- Buyer may cancel before dispatch with no penalty
- Once dispatched, neither can cancel — only return

## 13. Disputes

- Buyer files dispute within **14 days of delivery**
- Both parties submit evidence within **48 hours**
- Any & All mediates; decision is final and binding
- Repeated disputes against a seller → suspension

## 14. Seller Ratings & Suspension

- Buyers rate seller after each purchase
- Sellers with rating < 4.0 over 10+ orders → coached
- Sellers with rating < 3.0 over 20+ orders → suspended

Immediate suspension triggers:
- Selling banned products
- Counterfeit goods
- Repeated non-delivery
- Harassment of buyers
- Bypassing platform (asking buyers to pay outside)

## 15. Going Live (Live Shows)

- Seller schedules a show in Seller Hub
- Adds product list with starting bids and Buy It Now prices
- Goes live on scheduled time
- Buyers bid in real time
- Highest bidder wins when seller closes auction

## 16. Communication

- All buyer-seller comms must happen on platform (we record for safety)
- No exchange of phone numbers or external links during live shows
- Sellers caught taking buyers off-platform → suspension

## 17. Taxes

- Seller is responsible for their own GST, income tax filings
- Any & All issues monthly statement of sales for accounting
- TDS deduction may apply per Income Tax Act
